Working For You - All Year Round

Rugby Liberal Democrats are passionate about making Rugby a better, safer and fairer place for everyone. We work hard all year around, not just at election times!

If you need our help with a local issue or campaign, don't hesitate to contact us. If you are interested in joining us, there are many ways to get involved.

Please help us to do more in your area by giving us your vote during local elections. Every vote helps, so make sure yours counts.

  • Nicola Davies calls on PCC candidates to join her & sign the ethical standards checklist
    Article: Apr 16, 2016
    Read More
    By Dale Keeling

    Nicola Davies, has vowed to deliver an 'open, transparent and ethical' approach across the Warwickshire force if elected.

    The Committee on Standards in Public Life calls on the next police boss to publish and abide by an ethical code of conduct. It also urges them to enforce a transparent process during the appointment of the chief constable, as well as key staff. Nicola Davies has confirmed she intends to make accessing data and information on the commissioner's website 'easier and more relevant'. She added: "Transparency, confidence and consent are critical factors in effective and efficient policing. "I am willing to openly publish data about my pay and benefits. "I will publish all non-confidential letters between my office and the Home Office, Treasury and local government so that you can see, openly and publicly, how we hold each other to account. "And I will seek to ensure that 'confidentiality' is not used to hide and obscure communications and public information. "I equally believe that Warwickshire residents need to know that appointments I make are the result of choosing the best people for the role. Merit and effectiveness must remain the key requirements. "I call on the other candidates to do the same."
